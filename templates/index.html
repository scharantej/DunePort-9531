Dune: Imperium

Dune: Imperium is a strategy game set in the Dune universe. Players take on the roles of noble houses vying for control of the planet Arrakis, the only known source of the valuable spice melange.

The game combines deckbuilding, worker placement, and area control elements to create a unique and challenging experience. Players must carefully manage their resources, build their armies, and forge alliances to achieve their goals.
